Support RetroWeb

RetroWeb is a **community-driven** initiative dedicated to bringing back the aesthetics, functionality, and nostalgia of the early 2000s internet. **Your support helps us maintain RetroWeb, develop new content, and ensure free access for everyone**—forever.

Why Support RetroWeb?

  • Preserve Internet History: We’re committed to archiving and reviving the web's early golden days.
  • 100% Free for Everyone: Unlike modern platforms, we **do not charge** users to explore RetroWeb.
  • No Intrusive Ads: We keep our platform **ad-free** for a clean, immersive experience.
  • Community-Oriented: Your contributions allow us to create new features based on what **you** want.

How Your Donation Helps

Every contribution—big or small—makes a difference:

  • **Website Hosting & Maintenance** – Keep RetroWeb online & stable.
  • **Development & Content** – Funding articles, guides, and software updates.
  • **Server & Security Upgrades** – Ensuring RetroWeb remains **safe** and **accessible worldwide**.
