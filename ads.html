Ads Policy

At RetroWeb, we are committed to providing a clean, user-friendly, and distraction-free browsing experience. This Ads Policy outlines our approach to advertising, ensuring that all displayed ads align with our core values of transparency, quality, and user privacy. Our goal is to strike a balance between delivering relevant advertisements and maintaining an enjoyable experience for our visitors.

  • Relevance: We carefully select advertisements that align with our users' interests and browsing preferences. Ads are tailored to enhance rather than disrupt the user experience.
  • Transparency: All advertisements are clearly labeled as "Sponsored" or "Advertisement" to distinguish them from our organic content. We do not engage in deceptive advertising practices.
  • Quality: We maintain high standards for the ads displayed on RetroWeb. Advertisements must be professional, non-intrusive, and free from misleading, offensive, or inappropriate content.
  • Privacy & Data Protection: We take user privacy seriously. RetroWeb does not share personal data with advertisers without explicit user consent. All advertisements comply with our strict privacy guidelines and adhere to data protection laws.
  • Ad Frequency & Placement: Ads are displayed in moderation to prevent an overwhelming or disruptive experience. We ensure that advertisements do not interfere with the readability of our content.
  • Third-Party Advertising: We collaborate with trusted and reputable third-party ad networks to serve relevant and secure advertisements. Our partners are carefully vetted to maintain the integrity of our platform.
  • User Control & Feedback: Users have control over their ad preferences. They can opt out of personalized advertising through their account settings or provide feedback on ads they find intrusive or irrelevant.
  • Legal Compliance: We adhere to all applicable advertising regulations, including consumer protection laws and data privacy policies, to ensure ethical and responsible ad placement.

Our commitment to an ad-friendly yet user-centric approach ensures that advertisements on RetroWeb remain relevant, respectful, and non-intrusive. If you have any questions or concerns about our advertising policy, please feel free to reach out to us.
