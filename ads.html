Ads Policy

At RetroWeb, we are committed to providing a clean, user-friendly, and distraction-free browsing experience. This Ads Policy outlines our approach to advertising, ensuring that all displayed ads align with our core values of transparency, quality, and user privacy.

Our Advertising Principles

  • Relevance: We carefully select advertisements that align with our users' interests.
  • Transparency: Ads are clearly labeled as "Sponsored" or "Advertisement".
  • Quality: Ads must be professional, non-intrusive, and free from misleading content.
  • Privacy & Data Protection: We do not share personal data with advertisers without user consent.
  • Ad Frequency & Placement: Ads are displayed in moderation to maintain readability.
  • Third-Party Advertising: We collaborate only with trusted third-party ad networks.
  • User Control & Feedback: Users can opt out of personalized advertising.
  • Legal Compliance: We adhere to all applicable advertising regulations.
  • No Pop-Ups or Intrusive Ads: We do not allow disruptive ad formats.
  • Selective Ad Networks: We partner only with reputable ad networks.
  • Ad-Free Subscription Option: A future ad-free subscription is planned.
  • Ethical Advertising: Ads for harmful or illegal services are prohibited.
  • Ad Performance Monitoring: We continuously monitor ad effectiveness.
  • No Data Selling: RetroWeb does not sell user data to advertisers.
  • Transparency Reports: We periodically publish ad transparency reports.
  • No Flash Ads: We do not allow outdated Flash-based advertisements.
  • Load Time Optimization: Ads are optimized for fast loading speeds.
  • Advertiser Accountability: We hold advertisers accountable for misleading claims.
  • Strict Content Review: Ads undergo multiple quality checks before approval.
  • Ads Aligned with Content: We ensure ads match the theme and tone of our platform.

If you have any questions or concerns about our Ads Policy, please contact us at Email Address: info@retroweb.space.
